EduTECH Africa 2019

Johannesburg, South Africa

Home Admission

EduTECH Africa is Africa's largest conference & exhibition covering the whole education lifecycle and is the leading education exhibition on the continent.

Technology is the driving enerey behind a change in the fundamental purpose and delivery of education. Today’s students need to be equipped with a set of 21st-century skills that prepare them for tomorrow’s workplace.

We understand that it’s hard to keep up with the continual development of new technologies, innovative learning experiences and ways of measuring student mastery. This is where EduTECH Africa steps in.

Over the last five years, EduTECH Africa has grown to become the largest and most influential EdTech conference and exhibition in Africa. At EduTECH Africa prides itself in bringing together thousands of educators from across Africa to evaluate and plan the future of technology in learning. On the 9th – 10th of October 2019, EduTECH Africa will gather the region’s leading minds in education to harness the power of technology and drive 21st-century learning. eTraverse is exhibiting in its very first year from its inception at the startup pavilion on stand number 83.
