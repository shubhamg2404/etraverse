About Us

Do you still remember the moment, when for the very first time you were encouraged. That spark and the bliss you had, when you wanted to create something original, superior, relatable yet exceptional? That’s the same passion ignited when we created eTraverse.

eTraverse flagged in August 2018 to stimulate learners, support teachers and management, and promote eLearning excellence. We were encouraged to use the power of technology to build interactive and collaborative learning and administrative solutions for African continent, and at the very same time, we wanted to reach everyone out there who would get benefitted from our EduTech solutions?

eTraverse is a one-stop solution for all your eduTech needs. We have in- depth knowledge and skillful expertise in developing a wide range of custom eLearning and administrative solutions with exceptional effectiveness and appropriate strategies.

Vision

eTraverse was incepted in 2018 with a clear vision to provide end to end eduTech solutions that empower the African Education Industry with outcome-driven administrative and eLearning solutions.

Mission

We are on a mission to design, develop, and deploy technology-enhanced interactive eduTech solutions to make learning engaging and entertaining.
