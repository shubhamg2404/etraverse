What we do

eTraverse works collaboratively across the South African Education Fraternity to build affordable and convenient digital learning and management solutions that make learning more involving and enriching by creating an eLearning environment that’s perfectly right for educators and learners.

At eTraverse, we are committed to addressing your learning challenges though our custom eLearning and administrative solutions that are innovative, interactive, and bespoke, perfectly aligned to meet your organizational goals.

About Us

Do you still remember the moment, when for the very first time you were encouraged? That spark and the bliss you had, when you wanted to create something original, superior, relatable yet exceptional? That’s the same passion ignited when we created eTraverse.

eTraverse flagged in August 2018 to stimulate learners, support teachers, and promote eLearning excellence. We were encouraged to use the power of technology to build interactive and collaborative learning solutions for the African continent, and at the very same time, we wanted to reach everyone out there who would get benefitted from our eLearning solutions.

eTraverse is a one-stop solution for all your eLearning needs. We have in- depth knowledge and skillful expertise in developing a wide range of custom eLearning solutions with exceptional eLearning effectiveness and appropriate learner strategies.

We are on the verge to set new standards in creating eLearning solutions.

Vision

eTravese was incepted in 2018 with a clear vision to provide end to end eLearning solutions that empower the African Learning community with outcome-driven eLearning courses.

Mission

We are on a mission to design, develop, and deploy technology-enhanced interactive eLearning solutions to make learning engaging and entertaining.
