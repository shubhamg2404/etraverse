Library managment System

Managing a Library involves hundreds of tasks. The work of a librarian is not at all easy. Librarian has a number of responsibilities to fulfill that includes, a specific book search, book issue, late fine and a log of several other activities. Owing to these and numbers of other reasons, today school and universities looking Library Management System Application to address all the library needs and measures.

At eTraverse, we have highly integrated, user-friendly, and compatible Library Management System to manage all the library resources in the most effective and effective manner of any size or type of library. Our LMS can run on both the mode web-based and local host-based. Upon your specific business requirements, we also make it available on all the latest technologies that include; smartphone, tablet, cloud hosting, SMS, email, UHF RFID, payment gateway, etc.

Our School/University Library Management Software Includes Following Operations

  1. Knowledge Management
  2. Catalog Management
  3. Barcode Scanning
  4. Acquisition Management
  5. Barcoding / RFID
  6. Circulation Management
  7. Fee Collection
  8. Patron Management
  9. Reserve Shelf Management
  10. Search Functionality
  11. Self Check-in / Check-Out
  12. Serials Management
  13. Kiosk Integration
